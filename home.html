Our Journey
Start College Journey
We walked in as strangers in June 2023, not knowing these classrooms would turn into memories we’d carry forever.
JUNE 2023
First day of college — one simple question, “Why don’t you use bad words?” started our first real conversation and broke the silence.
OCTOBER 2023
Aayudha Pooja — our class celebrated louder than everyone, standing out in the entire college with pure energy and fun.
JANUARY 2024
Pongal celebration — games, decorations, and full enjoyment in our block, making it one of the happiest days together.
JUNE 2024
Promoted to second year — new confidence, new classroom, and a stronger bond as a batch.
OCTOBER 2024
Aayudha Pooja — while others celebrated, we were stuck in the lab with 8085 practicals, watching from inside.
JANUARY 2025
Pongal — our class stood out with unique outfits, grabbing everyone’s attention across the campus.
FEBRUARY 2025
Annual Day — our class dance stole the show, and the entire college talked about our performance.
APRIL 2025
Department function — food, fun, and speeches made it feel like a small festival in our department.
JUNE 2025
Entered third year — stronger, more confident, and closer than ever as a batch.
JULY 2025
Juniors joined — we became seniors, guiding them and feeling the responsibility for the first time.
DECEMBER 2025
Industrial Visit — a 3-day trip full of fun, but we missed the friends who couldn’t join in every moment.
JANUARY 2026
Final year began — new connections, more bonding, and a different feeling knowing this was our last phase.
APRIL 13, 2026
A day at a friend’s house — cooking, laughing, photos, and memories that turned into one unforgettable day.
APRIL 2026
Farewell — laughter, tears, and the last hug that said everything… this was the end.

Final exams — CN, .NET, IoT, HCI, PHP… the last step before we walked out of college forever.
